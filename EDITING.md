# How to Edit Your Website

This guide is for you, Bonnie. No coding required.

## Getting Started

1. Open your email and find the Pages CMS invitation
2. Click the magic link — you're logged in
3. You'll see your website content organized by section

Bookmark the editing page so you can get back to it easily: [app.pagescms.org](https://app.pagescms.org)

## Making an Edit

1. Click on **Website Content** to open the editor
2. Find the section you want to change (Hero, Pricing, About, etc.)
3. Edit the text in the field
4. Click **Save** at the top

Your change goes live in about 60 seconds. Refresh your site to see it.

## Updating Photos

1. In Pages CMS, look for the image fields
2. Click to upload a new image
3. Select your file — it gets added to the site automatically

Keep images under 1MB for fast loading. JPG for photos, PNG for graphics.

## Tips

- **Preview before saving:** Read your changes carefully. Once you save, the site updates automatically.
- **One change at a time:** Make a small edit, save, check the site. Then make the next edit. This makes it easy to undo if something looks wrong.
- **Special characters:** If you want a non-breaking space (keeps two words together on the same line), type `&nbsp;` between them. Example: `the right&nbsp;fit` keeps "right fit" from splitting across lines.
- **Bold text in About section:** The belief statement field allows basic HTML. Use `<strong>text</strong>` to bold words.

## If Something Goes Wrong

**The site looks broken after an edit:**
Don't panic. Go to [github.com/whale/bonnie](https://github.com/whale/bonnie) and click "commits." Find the most recent one (your edit) and let Matthew know. He can revert it in seconds.

**You can't log in to Pages CMS:**
Check your email for a new magic link, or ask Matthew to resend an invitation.

**Pages CMS is down:**
This is rare, but if the editing tool itself isn't loading, the live site is unaffected. Your site stays up. Just wait and try again later, or let Matthew know.
